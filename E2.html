📖 Texto de lectura

My name is Emma. I am 22 years old and I live in a small town called Greenfield. I work as a waitress at a café called "The Sunny Spoon". The café is near the park, and it opens at 7:00 am from Monday to Saturday. On Sundays, the café is closed.

Every morning, I wake up at 5:30 am. I take a shower, have breakfast, and then I walk to work. It takes me 20 minutes to walk to the café. My favorite part of the job is talking to customers. Many customers are friendly and they always say "good morning" to me.

Yesterday was a special day. A famous singer came to the café. His name is Leo. He ordered a cup of coffee and a piece of chocolate cake. I was very nervous, but he was very kind. He said "thank you" and gave me a big tip. After he left, all my colleagues asked me about him. I will never forget that day!

In my free time, I like reading mystery novels and watching movies. I live with my cat, Whiskers. He is black and white and very playful. On weekends, I visit my parents. They live in the next town, about 30 minutes by bus. I love my simple life.
